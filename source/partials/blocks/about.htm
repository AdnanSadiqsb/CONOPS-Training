About CONOPS Training

At CONOPS Training, we deliver professional security and safety training built on real operational experience. Our instructors bring over 50 years of combined service across the British Army, private security, and civilian operational roles.

We specialise in practical, scenario-based training designed to prepare learners for real-world environments — not just classroom theory. Our experience spans Close Protection, Door Supervision, Conflict Management, Physical Intervention, and multiple First Aid disciplines.

Every course is structured to build confidence, competence, and professionalism, ensuring learners leave ready to meet the demands of today’s security industry.
